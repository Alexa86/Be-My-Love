Mumbai → Chennai • A Love Story
Chapter II • The Passage

Through India

The journey became a meditation. Each passing mile brought me closer to you, yet the distance felt infinite. Through cities that never sleep and villages frozen in time, I carried one thought.

I thought about your passion for exploring the world—how your eyes light up when speaking of distant places, untold stories, cultures yet to be discovered. This journey was mine, but I imagined it as ours.

Every sunset I witnessed alone, I saved for us to see together. Every story I heard, I stored away to share with you. Because travel isn't about the miles covered—it's about who you wish was beside you.

"Home isn't where you start. It's where your heart finally stops searching."
Chapter III • The Discovery

Chennai

I arrived at dawn, when the city was still waking. The scent of filter coffee and fresh bread guided me through unfamiliar streets until I found what I was looking for.

A small space where flour becomes art. Where you stand, lost in concentration, transforming simple ingredients into moments of joy. Watching you work was like witnessing devotion in motion.

Your passion for baking isn't just about recipes or technique. It's about creating something from nothing, about the patience to wait for dough to rise, the intuition to know when something is ready.

In that moment, I understood: you don't just bake. You craft experiences. You create memories that linger long after the last bite.

A Thought
The way you measure ingredients with such care reminded me that love, too, requires precision. Not in controlling every detail, but in knowing exactly what matters.
Chapter IV • The Rhythm

Every Heartbeat

After 1,300 kilometers, standing here in Chennai, I've come to understand something fundamental about the human heart.

It doesn't beat for destinations. It beats for people. For moments. For the promise of something that makes every mile worthwhile.

Listen
This is the rhythm that carried me across India. Every beat whispers your name.
My heart has been beating for you long before I made this journey. It will continue beating for you long after. The question now is whether yours might join mine in the same rhythm.
